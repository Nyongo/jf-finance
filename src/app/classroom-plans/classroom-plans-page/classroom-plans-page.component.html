About Our Classroom Plans

Standard Classroom Plans

At Jackfruit Finance, we understand that building a high-quality educational environment for students is about more than just providing funding.

That's why, as part of our service, every Jackfruit Finance school gains access to our database of stock project plans. If you want to construct one of these plans, Jackfruit can handle every element of the project including:

  • Design
  • Permitting
  • Financing
  • Construction

Standard Classroom Plans

Our stock project plans have been designed with one goal in mind: to create the best possible learning environment for students. Our team of experts has considered every aspect of school design, from the materials used to build classrooms, to the lighting, electricals, and ventilation. All of our plans are designed to meet international standards, ensuring that students have access to safe, healthy and well-equipped classrooms.

With access to our stock project plans, schools no longer have to rely on informal local workers who may not have the experience or knowledge needed to build the best possible environment. Instead, they can choose from a range of proven designs that have been tested and approved, giving them the confidence that their classrooms will be of the highest quality.

In addition, our stock project plans can also help schools save time and money. By having access to pre-approved designs, schools can avoid the delays that often come with local government approvals, ensuring that their classrooms are built quickly and efficiently. We have also partnered with a construction firm to ensure the classrooms are built quickly and within budget, with the aim of getting them ready to use as quickly as possible.
