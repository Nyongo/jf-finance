Our Mission

Private education in Kenya is growing every year, and for many families, even those on the lowest incomes, it is the only way of securing an education for their children.

However, we know that schools struggle to qualify for affordable financing from traditional lenders. If your school doesn't have the necessary financial reports in place to meet the application criteria, you probably can't even apply for a loan. Jackfruit has changed this, offering rapid loans with flexible criteria to enable you to expand and develop your school, and get more children learning.
