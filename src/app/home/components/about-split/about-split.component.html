About Jackfruit Finance

Affordable Financing For Schools.

Every child should have access to an education. It doesn’t matter where they live or how much their family earns, all children have the right to learn.

Jackfruit Finance improves children’s access to education by helping schools. We offer affordable, accessible loans to private schools across Kenya. Loans that can build classrooms, buy school buses, and enable schools to improve their learning environment.
