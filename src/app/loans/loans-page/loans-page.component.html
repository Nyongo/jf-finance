ABOUT OUR LOANS

Jackfruit Finance Loans

Jackfruit Finance has two funding options for schools.

Project and Asset Loans

Amount: Up to 30 million KES
Tenure: 9-60 months
Collateral: Required

Can be used for:

  • Building/expanding schools
  • Repair and maintenance
  • Playgrounds and labs
  • Buying textbooks and supplies
  • Changing legal status
  • Buying buses and vehicles
  • Purchasing land
  • Financing other schools

Working Capital

Amount: Up to 500,000 KES
Tenure: 2-12 months
Collateral: Not required

Can be used for:

  • Teacher salaries
  • Textbooks and supplies

All loans are subject to credit checks. Contact us for more details.

Partner Rewards

Jackfruit schools also receives free rewards during the period of the loan, in addition to access to our partners who provide other programs and benefits at low cost.

All Schools Receive

  • A Jackfruit wall clock as a welcome gift

Project and Asset Loan Schools Receive

  • Water purification tablets and 100 liter water tanks
  • 10 spaces in the Jackfruit After School program
  • 2,000 KES of textbooks every December
